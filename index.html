ABOUT US

Welcome to Telangana Home, where we're not just serving delicious food, but also nourishing dreams and aspirations. Our mission is simple yet profound: to create a hunger-free Telangana while simultaneously addressing the issue of child hunger. At Telangana Home, we believe that no child should go to bed hungry, and every child deserves a chance at a brighter future.

OUR STORY

Telangana Home began as a humble food truck business with a vision to make a difference. We realized that the power to transform lives lies not only in the flavors we serve but also in the opportunities we provide. Our journey started with the aim of reducing child hunger and has since evolved into something much greater.

Hunger-Free Telangana

Our primary goal is to ensure that no one in Telangana goes to bed on an empty stomach. We offer a range of delicious, nutritious, and affordably priced meals that cater to diverse tastes and preferences. Whether you're looking for a quick snack or a hearty meal, Telangana Home has something for everyone.

Educate Children

We firmly believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. That's why a portion of our proceeds goes toward providing education and hostel facilities to underprivileged children. By dining at Telangana Home, you are contributing to the education and future of these children.

Community Support

Telangana Home isn't just a food truck; it's a community of like-minded individuals who share the belief that change is possible. We actively engage with our community through various initiatives, food drives, and outreach programs to uplift those in need.

Join Us in Making a Difference

When you choose Telangana Home for your meals, you're not just satisfying your hunger; you're becoming a part of a movement to eliminate child hunger and empower the future generation. Join us on this journey towards a hunger-free Telangana and a brighter tomorrow for all.
